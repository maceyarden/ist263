Mini Review

Overall: The house-made pasta has the perfect bite, and the sauce tastes slow-simmered and balanced. The star, though, is the Spicy Hot Tomato Oil — sweet heat with a garlicky kick.

Portions are generous without feeling heavy, and service is upbeat even at peak hours. Expect a short wait on weekends; it moves quickly and feels worth it.

Need to Know

  • 311 S Franklin St, Syracuse, NY
  • Price: $$
  • Best for: Dates, parents’ weekend, pasta cravings
  • Must-order: Hot Tomato Oil + bread
