Mini Review

Overall: The house-made pasta has perfect bite, and the slow-simmered sauce is rich without feeling heavy. The true star, though, is the Spicy Hot Tomato Oil — garlicky, sweet heat that keeps you dipping until the basket’s gone.

The portions are generous and the energy inside is upbeat even on weekends. Expect a short wait during peak hours — but it moves quickly and always feels worth it.

Need to Know

  • 311 S Franklin St, Syracuse, NY
  • Price: $$
  • Perfect For: Dates, parent visits, pasta cravings
  • Don’t Miss: Hot Tomato Oil + bread
