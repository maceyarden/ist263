Dinosaur BBQ

★★★★★

BBQ • $$ • Downtown

Top Dishes

  • St. Louis Ribs — meaty, smoky, perfectly charred.
  • Pulled Pork Sandwich — pile it high, add slaw for crunch.
  • Mac ‘n’ Cheese — peppery, creamy, crowd favorite.

Quick Tips

  • Arrive early on weekends — lines move but get long.
  • Try a sauce flight for extra fun.
  • Great for groups — share platters stretch your dollars.

Alto Cinco

★★★☆☆

Mexican / Southwestern • $–$$ • Westcott

Top Dishes

  • Burritos & Tacos — go wild on salsas; add guac.
  • Vegan Nachos — huge serving, great for sharing.
  • Fish Tacos — crisp, limey, perfect texture.

Quick Tips

  • Open late on weekends — expect traffic.
  • Good takeout spot for study nights.
  • Tiny tables — arrive early with groups.
