Dinosaur BBQ

★★★★★
BBQ • $$ • Downtown

Top Dishes

  • St. Louis Ribs — Meaty, balanced smoke, good char.
  • Pulled Pork Sandwich — Pile it high; add slaw for crunch.
  • Mac ’n’ Cheese — Creamy, peppery, crowd favorite.

Quick Tips

  • Go early on weekends to avoid the line.
  • Ask for extra napkins and try a sauce flight.
  • Great for groups — share platters stretch your dollars.
