A Syracuse icon known for fresh pasta and its cult-favorite Spicy Hot Tomato Oil. It’s cozy, lively, great for date nights or family dinners.

Full Review

Review: The house-made pasta has the perfect bite, and the sauce tastes slow-simmered and balanced. The star, though, is the Spicy Hot Tomato Oil — sweet heat with a garlicky finish that keeps you dipping until the basket’s gone.

Portions are generous without feeling heavy, and service is upbeat even at peak hours. Expect a short wait on weekends; it moves quickly and feels worth it.

Best seats are near the windows for natural light. Pair pasta with a crisp soda or sparkling water to cut the richness.
